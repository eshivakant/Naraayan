Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need to provide for a consultation?

You'll need your complete birth details: date of birth, exact time of birth (as accurate as possible), and place of birth. For specific consultations, please also mention your area of concern (career, relationship, health, etc.).

How long does it take to receive my reading?

Turnaround time depends on your package: Quick Insight (12 hours), Focused Consultation (24-36 hours), Video Consultations (scheduled within 48 hours), and comprehensive packages (48-72 hours). Rush service available upon request.

What is Lal Kitab and how is it different?

Lal Kitab is a unique system of Vedic astrology that provides simple, practical, and highly effective remedies. Unlike traditional methods, Lal Kitab remedies are easy to implement in daily life and often show quicker results. They focus on karmic debt resolution and practical solutions.

Can I ask follow-up questions after my consultation?

Yes! All packages include one round of clarification questions via email/WhatsApp. For video consultations, you can ask questions during the session. Extended support packages are also available.

Are the remedies complicated or expensive?

Not at all! Lal Kitab remedies are specifically designed to be simple, affordable, and practical. Most remedies involve everyday items and activities that easily fit into modern lifestyles. No expensive rituals or complex procedures.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

I stand behind the quality of my consultations. If you're not satisfied with your reading, please contact me within 48 hours and I'll work with you to address your concerns or provide a partial refund on a case-by-case basis.
